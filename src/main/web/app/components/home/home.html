The ARMS data portal enables data collection at the source (in the field):

  1. Generate the spreadsheet templates. Some fields are mandatory but you can choose from a variety of fields to include in the template.
  2. Enter your ARMS metadata on the spreadsheet. Make sure to respect the standards described in the spreadsheet.
  3. Validate your spreadsheet in the data portal to make sure the fields don’t contain any errors. Choose the excel spreadsheet that contains your ARMS deployment information and click on “Validate”. Errors will appear in red and require to be addressed before you can upload your data sheet. Warnings will refer to other problems that will not interfere with the upload process.
  4. Once the spreadsheet has been successfully validated, you will have the possibility to upload it. Choose the validated spreadsheet, and either “create a new project” if this is the first time you are uploading the data or select an existing project to update the existing data.
  5. Visualize and manage your data in “Manage Data”
